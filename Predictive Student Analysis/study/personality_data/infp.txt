Overview:
	You, more than others iNtuitive Feeling types, are focused on making the world a better place for people. Their primary goal is to find out their meaning in life. What is their purpose? How can they best serve humanity in their lives? They are idealists and perfectionists, who drive themselves hard in their quest for achieving the goals they have identified for themselves.
Strength:
	1.	Idealistic-
			Your friends and loved ones will come to admire and depend on you for your optimism.
	2.	Seek and Value Harmony-
			People of your type have no interest in having power over others, and don’t much care for domineering attitudes at all.
	3.	Open-Minded and Flexible-
			A live-and-let-live attitude comes naturally to you, and you dislike being constrained by rules.
	4.	Very Creative-
			You combine their intuitive nature with their open-mindedness to allow them to see things from unconventional perspectives
	5.	Passionate and Energetic-
			When something captures INFPs’ imagination and speaks to their beliefs, they go all in, dedicating their time, energy, thoughts and emotions to the project.
Weakness:
	1.	Too Idealistic-
			You often take their idealism too far, setting themselves up for disappointment as, again and again, evil things happen in the world.
	2.	Too Altruistic-
			You sometimes see yourself as selfish, but only because you want to give so much more than you are able to.
	3.	Dislike Dealing With Data-
	4.	Impractical-
			When something captures your imagination, you can neglect practical matters like day-to-day maintenance and simple pleasures.
